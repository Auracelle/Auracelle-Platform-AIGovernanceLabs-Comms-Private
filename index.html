IP & Legal Hygiene

Protecting E-AGPO-HT & E-AGSO-HT

The E-AGPO-HT and E-AGSO-HT frameworks are wholly owned by Auracelle AI Governance Labs and were developed prior to and independently of any academic enrollment. This distinction is critical: Bath Spa University's IP policy applies to work created using university resources or in the course of university study — not to prior independent work.

Before commercial engagements proceed, ensure the following hygiene items are addressed:

Document the development timeline of E-AGPO-HT and E-AGSO-HT with timestamped records (GitHub commits, dated drafts, emails) establishing pre-enrollment provenance
Review Bath Spa's IP policy carefully — specifically the clause on prior work and independently developed IP — and consider seeking written confirmation that Auracelle systems fall outside university IP claims
Never refer to Bath Spa University in commercial or consulting contexts — this creates ambiguity about IP ownership that sophisticated clients and funders will notice
Establish a clear licensing model before the first paid engagement: what are you licensing, to whom, under what terms, with what exclusivity provisions
GitHub repos: ensure the public-facing code does not expose the proprietary scoring constructs, stratum architecture, or NOF parameterization that constitute the core IP
All consulting engagements should be executed under Auracelle AI Governance Labs — not under your personal name — to establish the entity as the IP holder
